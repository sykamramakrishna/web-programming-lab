Emergency Helpline Numbers in India

Police Emergency

Police helpline for reporting crimes, theft, violence or emergencies requiring police help.

Phone: 100

Email: police@india.gov.in

Medical Emergency (Ambulance)

Ambulance service for medical emergencies and transport to hospitals.

Phone: 102

Email: ambulance@health.gov.in

Fire Emergency

Fire brigade service for fire accidents and rescue operations.

Phone: 101

Email: fire@india.gov.in

Women Safety Helpline

Helpline for women safety and reporting harassment or abuse.

Phone: 1091

Email: womenhelp@india.gov.in

Child Helpline

Helpline for children needing care, protection or emergency support.

Phone: 1098

Email: childhelp@india.gov.in

Disaster Management

Emergency help during disasters such as floods, earthquakes and cyclones.

Phone: 108

Email: disaster@ndma.gov.in

Senior Citizen Helpline

Support and protection helpline for senior citizens.

Phone: 14567

Email: seniorhelp@india.gov.in

Road Accident Emergency

Emergency help for road accidents and highway incidents.

Phone: 1033

Email: highwayhelp@india.gov.in

Railway Helpline

Helpline for passenger safety and railway travel emergencies.

Phone: 139

Email: railwayhelp@indianrail.gov.in
